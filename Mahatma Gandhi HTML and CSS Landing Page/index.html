Mahatma Gandhi

About him

☛ Mahatma Gandhi, a towering figure in the history of India and the world, embodied the principles of nonviolent resistance and civil disobedience in his pursuit of justice and freedom.
☛ Born on October 2, 1869, in Porbandar, India, Gandhi's life was dedicated to challenging injustice through peaceful means.

☛ His philosophy of Satyagraha, or truth-force, inspired millions to stand against oppression with courage and integrity.

☛ Through acts of peaceful protest, fasting, and self-sacrifice, Gandhi led India to independence from British rule in 1947, leaving a legacy that continues to resonate globally as a beacon of hope for those seeking social change through nonviolence.
